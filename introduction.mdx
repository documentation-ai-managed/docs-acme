## Start here&#x20;

1. Create account from dashboard

2. Configure your project.

3. Customize your onboarding flow in the [Quickstart](/quickstart) page.

4. Delete or rewrite any sections and pages you do not plan to use.

## Make your first edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rewrite this page" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Update the title, description, and sections in `introduction` so they reflect your product and documentation goals.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Quickstart" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Replace the steps in `quickstart` with your real onboarding flow so new readers know exactly how to get started.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove or repurpose samples" icon="trash" title-type="p">
    Delete unused sample sections and pages, or rewrite them with your own content to keep navigation focused.
  </Step>
</Steps>
